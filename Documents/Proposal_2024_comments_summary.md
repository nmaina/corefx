## Nelly Maina — selected comments (2024 doc)

- **Outcomes:** Clarify virologic (suppression and failure) and clinical (how we define: active on treatment, ITT, death, OI, transfer out).
- **Death data:** "@dgaitho@ampath.or.ke we are missing data on Death to answer the clinical outcomes question" (assigned to Douglas Gaitho).
- **Drug resistance:** "Do we have drug resistance data for patients before changing regimen?"
- **EMR access:** "How many don't have how many have? What is meant by having access?"
- **Statistical analysis – critique:** Without sensitivity analyses, findings may be fragile; no subgroup evaluation for children/adolescents, high/low CD4, NRTI backbone. **Recommendations:** Conduct sensitivity analyses (vary VL thresholds; compare ITT vs as-treated; exclude missing adherence; pre-specify subgroup analyses).
- **Outcome:** "Is the outcome suppression alone? What about VL failure?"
- **Confounders:** Adjust for a comprehensive list (age, sex, CD4, VL, TB, ART history, facility type, comorbidities, adherence); consider propensity score methods (IPTW or matching); assess missing data, sensitivity analysis, multiple imputation; acknowledge residual confounding.
- **Descriptive study:** "adjust as this is a descriptive study" (Nelly, Oct 2025).
