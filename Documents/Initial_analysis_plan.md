# Initial analysis plan — Descriptive and comparison only

**Scope:** This is the **initial analysis** for the second-line ART Kenya study. No regression models (no Cox, no log-binomial, no Poisson). Analysis is **descriptive and comparison** only.

---

## What we will do

- **Descriptive:** Summarise the cohort and outcomes using counts, proportions, and tables (overall and by subgroup).
- **Comparison:** Compare groups by presenting outcomes **in each group** side by side (e.g. % viral suppression in DTG vs PI; % in each clinical status by regimen; same for TDF vs AZT vs ABC). No formal regression (no RR from log-binomial, no hazard ratios from Cox, no Poisson).

## What we will not do (in this initial analysis)

- **No Cox proportional hazards** (or other time-to-event regression).
- **No log-binomial regression** (or other model for RR).
- **No Poisson regression** with person-time.
- **No inferential modelling** — no regression coefficients, no confidence intervals from models. If any CIs are reported, they would be descriptive (e.g. CI for a single proportion), not from regression.

---

## Methods in practice

1. **Cohort description**  
   Table 1: N, demographics, regimen distribution, baseline CD4, WHO stage, follow-up, etc. (overall and by regimen anchor and by NRTI backbone where relevant).

2. **Virological outcomes**  
   Proportions (and counts) with viral suppression (&lt;200 copies/mL) overall and by DTG vs PI, and by TDF vs AZT vs ABC. Proportions with virological failure similarly.

3. **Clinical outcomes**  
   Proportions (and counts) in each status (active, LTFU, died, transferred out, other) overall and by regimen and by NRTI backbone.

4. **Comparison**  
   Comparison = presenting the same outcomes **by group** in tables or figures (e.g. one column per regimen or per NRTI backbone) so readers can compare. No regression-based comparison.

5. **Subgroups (descriptive)**  
   Where useful: describe outcomes by age group (children vs adults), baseline CD4 strata, or advanced HIV/TB, as **descriptive** breakdowns only.

6. **Follow-up and censoring (for clarity)**  
   T0 = date of second-line ART initiation. If reporting by regimen, censor at regimen switch (as per team agreement). Document in methods; no time-to-event model.

---

## Protocol wording (suggested)

For the **Statistical analysis** section, use wording along these lines:

*"This is an initial analysis. We will use **descriptive statistics** to summarise demographic and clinical characteristics and outcomes (viral suppression, clinical status) **overall** and **by** ART regimen (DTG vs PI) and **by** NRTI backbone (TDF, AZT, ABC). Results will be presented as counts and proportions (and, where appropriate, medians and IQR for continuous variables) in tables and figures. **Comparison** between groups will be done by presenting outcomes in each group side by side. **No regression models** (no log-binomial, Cox, or Poisson) will be used in this initial analysis. Follow-up starts at the date of second-line ART initiation (T0). For regimen-specific summaries, patients who switch regimen after second-line start will be censored at the time of switch."*
